Email Authentication

February 9, 2025

MarketingStar’s new automated email domain authentication feature simplifies and speeds up the process of verifying and authenticating your email domains. In under 5 minutes, your domain is verified without needing technical support or IT involvement, saving you valuable time and improving email deliverability. This automation removes manual steps, allowing you to focus on growing your campaigns.

Key Benefits

Automatic DNS Record Retrieval

With Domain Automation, once you add your domain, Marketing Star automatically generates and provides the necessary SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records. No guesswork, no manual errors.

Improved Email Deliverability

Authentication ensures that your messages reach inboxes, not spam folders. Domain Automation ensures these settings are applied correctly to maximize your sender reputation.

End-to-End Guided Setup

Our step-by-step process makes the entire experience smooth and stress-free

Time-Saving Configuration

No need to wait days for DNS changes. Our system verifies records automatically, ensuring faster campaign setup and deployment.

How Domain Authentication Automation Works

Steps to Configure a Domain

Access Channel Settings

  • Under the Preferences Module, go to Settings
  • Select Channel Settings, then choose Email and the SMTP option.

Add a New Domain

  • Click the Add (+) Icon to open the Add Domain page
  • Enter your custom domain and click Validate.

Validate the Domain

  • After validation, an Authoring Page appears. Click Continue
  • The system analyzes the domain and extracts provider details.

Authenticating and Configuring the Domain

Steps for Authentication

  • Enter your domain provider’s credentials
  • Receive and enter the OTP sent to your registered email or phone.
  • Click Continue for successful authentication

After Authentication:

  • A Success Page confirms the process.
  • The domain status shows In Progress on the Listing Page

Verifying and Finalizing the Domain

  • Wait for the verification process to complete
  • The status changes to Verified once the domain is ready
  • Receive a confirmation email to finalize the setup.
