Cookies are simple text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device by a website’sserver (“Cookies”).Each Cookie is unique to your web browser. It will contain some anonymous informationsuch as a unique identifier, website’s domain name, and some digits and numbers. The Website will utilize Cookies when you use the site (all unless it is required by applicable law to provide a separate consent to use such Cookies, and in which case we will use such Cookies only after we receive your separate consent to such use and subject to your right to withdraw such consent at any time)

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies remain on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while Session Cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser.We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the purposes set out below:

Essential Cookies

Type: Session Cookies

Purpose: These Cookies are essential to provide services available through the Website and to enable some of its features. They help to authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts. For example, these Cookies let us recognize that you have created an account and have logged into that account.

Functionality Cookies

Type: Persistent Cookies

Purpose: Functionality Cookies allows the operation of the site in accordance with the choices you make. For example, we will recognize your usernameor language preference and remember how you customized the site during future visits. They help to provide you with a more personal experience and to avoid you having to re-enter your preferences every time you use the Website.

Analytical Cookies

Type: Persistent Cookies

Purpose: These cookies enable us and third-party services to collect aggregated data for statistical purposes on how our visitors use the website. They track information about traffic to the Website and how users use the Website. The information collected is typically linked to a pseudonymous identifier associated with the device you use to access the Website and do not contain personal information such as names and email addresses.

If you want to restrict or block the cookies that are set by our Website or previously stored, you can do so through your browser setting. Alternatively, you can visit www.internetcookies.com, which contains comprehensive information on how to do this on a wide variety of browsers and devices. Deletion or blocking of some Cookies may limit your experience of the Website. It will also not prevent the Website from placing further Cookies on your device unless and until you adjust your settings.
