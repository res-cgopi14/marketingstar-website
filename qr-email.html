QR for Email

February 9, 2025

Introducing QR-to-email: Seamless interaction through personalized email engagement

QR-to-email enables effortless communication by combining the power of QR codes and email marketing. When customers scan the QR code, they are instantly prompted to send a pre-filled email directly to your brand’s inbox—reducing friction and increasing conversions.

Brands can immediately act on the incoming email—whether by delivering a discount code or responding to customer inquiries—resulting in higher engagement and conversion rates.

The benefits

Instant Engagement

  • Give customers the power to respond quickly—whether it’s submitting feedback, signing up, or redeeming offers—through real-time email interactions.

Friction-Free Opt-In Process

  • Forget long forms and tedious signup methods. QR Email provides a simple, fast way for customers to opt in or connect with your brand.

Cost-Effective Communication

  • No need for costly apps or complicated workflows. QR Email leverages existing tools (email clients) that are available to virtually everyone

How it works

Create a new QR communication and select QR email

Go to the Communication Module and select SDC. From there select QR as the communication type.

Provide Key Details

Enter the estimated audience count, the email ID customers need to respond to along with the pre-filled message

Generate the QR code

Click Generate, and your QR code is ready to be included in marketing materials—whether on product packaging, event posters, or online assets.
