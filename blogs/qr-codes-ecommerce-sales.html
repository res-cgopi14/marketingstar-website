From Scan to Sale: How QR Codes Increase E-Commerce Purchases

Rachel Goh

February 27, 2025

QR codes have the potential to drive e-commerce purchases effectively. Despite their existence for more than two decades, there is still room for SMBs to make greater use of them. These innovative two-dimensional codes serve as a powerful marketing tool for small businesses seeking to engage with customers on the go, as they can be easily scanned by a smartphone camera to unlock digital information.

Here are three ways QR codes can increase sales for your brand.

How can QR codes drive purchases?

Deep-link your QR codes directly to the conversion page

It is important to be direct with your customers and avoid wasting their time. Scanning a code that suggests a specific action or piece of information, only to end up on a homepage, can be frustrating and sour the overall customer experience.

QR codes can streamline the customer journey to a clear and direct call-to-action, improving the overall user experience and boosting the likelihood of a purchase.

For example, QR codes can be used to direct customers to product pages featuring discounted offers, easily review their abandoned shopping cart to encourage purchase completion, reorder pages for frequently bough items, offering one-scan convenience.

Enrich multichannel experiences for maximum conversion

Experience ecommerce brands know that selling products on multiple channels is the best way to generate awareness on product listings, reviews, and promotions. Studies show that businesses using a multichannel strategy witness a 287% increase in customer engagement compared to single-channel strategy.

Consider integrating QR codes with various channels to improve customer outreach and generate brand awareness. This could involve allowing audiences to access exclusive content via a landing page and email, enabling them to try on products at a pop-up store, or participate in giveaways on social media.

Gather data to personalize conversion journeys

Personalization drives results, with personalized campaigns generating 40% more revenue than generic alternatives.

Personalization requires understanding customers through data collection and QR Code surveys provide an efficient solution for collecting valuable feedback without the hassle of lengthy URLs or complex navigation. To effectively personalize experiences, you need accurate data collection. QR codes simplify this process by embedding surveys, feedback forms, or registration links directly into customer journeys.

Some use cases include:

  • Embed QR codes in email newsletters, invoices, or product packaging to collect post-purchase feedback.
  • Offer incentives for customers to complete surveys by scanning QR codes, providing insights into shopping behaviors and preferences.
  • Use data collected through QR codes to offer tailored product recommendations and discounts, increasing repeat purchases.
