FAQs

You can measure the effectiveness of your SMS campaigns using metrics such as open rates, click-through rates, conversion rates, response rates, and overall engagement.

With an average open rate of about 98%, SMS marketing is a low-hanging fruit for promoting all you have to offer and keeping your brand on the consumers’ radar.

A subscriber list requires the express consent of individual contacts, which you can obtain through opt-in forms and emails created via Marketing Star.

SMS and email marketing are two channels that work well together. You can deliver detailed information using emails, while SMS campaigns can be reserved for more time-sensitive communications. You can also alternate between the two channels to retarget the same audience if they fail to respond to your initial outreach on one channel.

SMS is a default channel that you can access after signing up for a Marketing Star account, Refer to this detailed guide for more information.

Each subscription includes a default number of SMS messages that you can send each month. Each communication that exceeds one of those limits incurs a consumable cost, charged on an individual unit basis. The exact consumable cost per unit depends on the country in which you operate. For more information, visit our pricing page.
