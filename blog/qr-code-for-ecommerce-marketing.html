From Scan to Sale: QR Code Marketing Strategies That Boost E-Commerce Purchases

Rachel Goh

February 27, 2025

QR codes have the potential to significantly boost online sales, making them a powerful asset in QR code marketing for small and medium-sized businesses. Even though QR codes have existed for more than two decades, many small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) are still underutilizing their full potential, especially when it comes to QR code for ecommerce marketing.

These innovative two-dimensional codes act as a seamless bridge between offline and online experiences. With just a quick scan using a smartphone camera, customers can instantly access product pages, discounts, reviews, or interactive content. This makes QR codes an essential marketing tool for small businesses aiming to improve customer engagement, streamline the buying journey, and drive more conversions.

Here are three effective QR code marketing strategies that can increase sales for your brand.

How Can QR Code Marketing Drive E-commerce Purchases?

Deep-link Your QR Codes Directly to the Conversion Page

It is important to be direct with your customers and avoid wasting their time. Scanning a code that suggests a specific action or piece of information, only to end up on a homepage, can be frustrating and sour the overall customer experience.

QR codes can streamline the customer journey to a clear and direct call-to-action, improving the overall user experience and boosting the likelihood of a purc

For example, QR codes can directly link shoppers to:

  • Product pages with exclusive or time-sensitive discounts
  • Abandoned cart pages to encourage purchase completion
  • Reorder pages for frequently bought items, allowing one-scan convenience

When implemented strategically, QR code marketing removes friction, delivers instant value, and drives faster e-commerce purchases.

Enrich Multichannel Experiences for Maximum Conversion

Experienced ecommerce brands know that selling products on multiple channels is the best way to generate awareness on product listings, reviews, and promotions. Studies show that businesses using a multichannel strategy witness a 287% increase in customer engagement compared to single-channel strategy.

Consider integrating QR codes with various channels to improve customer outreach and generate brand awareness. This could involve allowing audiences to access exclusive content via a landing page and email, enabling them to try on products at a pop-up store, or participate in giveaways on social media.

Gather Data to Personalize Conversion Journeys

Personalization drives results, with personalized campaigns generating 40% more revenue than generic alternatives.

Personalization requires understanding customers through data collection and QR Code surveys provide an efficient solution for collecting valuable feedback without the hassle of lengthy URLs or complex navigation. To effectively personalize experiences, you need accurate data collection. QR codes simplify this process by embedding surveys, feedback forms, or registration links directly into customer journeys.

Some Use Cases Include:

  • Embed QR codes in email newsletters, invoices, or product packaging to collect post-purchase feedback.
  • Offer incentives for customers to complete surveys by scanning QR codes, providing insights into shopping behaviors and preferences.
  • Use data collected through QR codes to offer tailored product recommendations and discounts, increasing repeat purchases.
