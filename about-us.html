About Marketing Star

Marketing Star is a streamlined multichannel marketing solution built for SMBs and businesses in the early stages of their digital journeys. The solution allows businesses to create, analyze, and manage marketing campaigns across email, SMS, QR codes, WhatsApp, and more from one user-friendly interface. Highly cost-effective, Marketing Star gives marketers a comprehensive mix of channels and features, while reducing their monthly fees through its pay-as-you-go model.

With a rich feature set for critical marketing use cases, brands will spend little time in getting their communications ready with creative approaches and better outcomes. Marketing Star was launched in 2024.

Our Vision

We are striving to empower small businesses across sectors to enjoy the immense value that automated multichannel marketing can generate. We do so through the development of practical, accessible features that are integrated, immediately impactful, and reflective of current marketing paradigms.
