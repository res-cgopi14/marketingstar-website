QR SMS

Introducing QR SMS:Your new core strategy to increase foot traffic

February 9, 2025

QR SMS is revolutionizing how businesses connect with customers by merging the simplicity of QR codes with the reach of SMS. With just a quick scan, customers are guided to their SMS app with a pre-filled personalized message to your brand, making it easy for them to opt in, redeem offers, or engage in real time—all with minimal friction.

The Benefits of QR SMS

Seamless and Instant Engagement

Customers interact with your brand in real time by simply scanning a QR code and hitting “Send” on a pre-filled SMS message. No forms, no delays—just instant action.

Cost-Effective Communication

No need for costly app downloads or complex sign-up processes. QR SMS works on any mobile device, making it accessible to all users.

Personalized, Friction-Free Experience

The pre-filled SMS is personalized and relevant, making customers feel valued while reducing drop-offs compared to traditional sign-up forms.

Higher Conversion Rates

By eliminating unnecessary steps in the opt-in or promotion redemption process, QR SMS can lead to better engagement and higher conversion rates.

How it works

  • Create an SDC Communication
  • Navigate to the QR communication module and select QR SMS
  • Set Up the Campaign Details
  • Enter the estimated audience count, the target phone number (where messages from users should be sent), and the pre-filled message that users will send. For example, the message could be: “Sign me up for updates!” or “Redeem 10% off.”
  • Generate the QR Code
  • The platform will automatically generate the unique QR code, ready for printing or digital distribution.

Take the First Step Toward Streamlined Customer Engagement

QR SMS removes friction, creates instant engagement, and delivers results. Let your customers interact with your brand like never before.
